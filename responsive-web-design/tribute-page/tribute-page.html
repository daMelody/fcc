Walter Piston


"A cylinder crankshift,
Nothing but net,
He studied Arnie's row,
And wrote for flute and clarinet,
A pseudo serialist
But a Crimson at heart
A Walt, but not Disney,
Two Pulitzers' for his art.""

-- Decoda Ensemble

Portrait of Walter Piston.

Walter Piston was an American composer of classical music, music theorist, and professor of music at Harvard University. Piston was born in Rockland, Maine. He died at home in Belmont, Massachusetts. He is the author of four books regarding music theory which have been utilized as recently as 2009.
